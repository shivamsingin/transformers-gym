Coach

Meet the mind behind Transformers Gym

Dr. Rohit Soni

Founder and Head Coach at Transformers Gym. Dr. Soni blends evidence‑based programming with real‑world experience from competitive stages to general population transformations.

Over the past decade, he has worked with beginners learning their first squat, busy professionals chasing sustainable fat‑loss, and athletes preparing for the stage. His approach is simple: master technique, program with purpose, and respect recovery. Every client receives a plan that fits their lifestyle—training volume suited to current capacity, nutrition that is realistic to execute, and checkpoints that make progress measurable. Dr. Soni is relentless about teaching the fundamentals: bracing, breathing, tempo, and positioning. Those habits compound quickly and keep you injury‑resilient as the loads go up and the physique changes.

Beyond the gym floor, he places equal emphasis on education. Members learn how to autoregulate sessions, when to push, and when to back off. Sleep, steps, and hydration are tracked as closely as sets and reps. Clients describe the experience as structured but flexible—hard work with clear intent and smart progressions. Whether the target is a first pull‑up, a photo‑shoot caliber transformation, or a return to pain‑free training, the process is the same: solid foundations, patient execution, and consistent accountability.
